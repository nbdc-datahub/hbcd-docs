Household Chemical Exposures: HBCD Modification Details

Removed Items

  • Items 1–2: Current Address & Length of Residence
  • Item 5: Is there an enclosed garage attached to this home or dwelling?
  • Items 7–7a: Does the child drink the tap water?
  • Items 13–13a: Is there a fume hood/fan over the cook stove or downdraft vent?
  • Items 18a–18d: How many days a week are the windows open for at least 2 hours a day?
  • Section B (Items 12–28): Entire section removed.

Modified Items

  • Item 1 Prompt
    "The following questions ask about exposure to secondhand smoke and the child's current home(s) or dwelling(s)."
    replaced with: "The following questions are about your child's primary residence (the place outside of daycare where they spend the most time)."
  • Item 14
    "Does this home or dwelling have any mold or mildew on walls or other surfaces other than in the shower or bathtub?"
    replaced with questions about: (1) water damage/dampness in the home during the past 12 months and (2) mildew or musty odors in the home.

New Items

  • Which fuels are used for heating the home? (multiple-choice checklist)
  • Is there any paint peeling or flaking outside the home?
  • Have there been repairs or renovations in the home during the past 12 months? If yes, which rooms?
